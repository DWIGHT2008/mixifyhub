Overview

The Recover Email feature helps users regain access to their accounts by attempting to retrieve the most recently used email address associated with a login attempt. This process is limited and depends on recent activity.

How It Works

  • Only the most recently used email is temporarily stored.
  • If a recent login was detected, that email is used for recovery.
  • If no match is found, the system redirects to registration.

Tips for Successful Recovery

  • Use the same device and browser you logged in with previously.
  • Ensure cookies or local storage are not cleared.
  • Try to recall the email or email clues used last.
